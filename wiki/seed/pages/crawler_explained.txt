====== How the Crawler Works ======

The crawler starts at one directory and walks downward.

For each entry it tries to answer questions like these:

  * What is the full path?
  * Is it a file, a directory, or a symbolic link?
  * What are the permissions?
  * How large is it?
  * Who owns it?
  * When was it last changed?

Those answers are turned into one database row.

If an entry cannot be read, the crawler logs the problem and continues.
